QR traceability

From scratch to full visibility.

Most organisations track nothing. Some track some things. We implement complete QR-based traceability — for any object, any process, any industry — built from the ground up so every touchpoint generates a permanent, queryable record.

01

Audit what exists

Map every asset, batch, document, or personnel touchpoint that currently has no trace record.

02

Design the traceability schema

Define what each QR scan captures: who, what, where, when, status, and chain of custody.

03

Generate and deploy QR codes

Custom QR labels printed and embedded at every critical node — production floor, lab, warehouse, field.

04

Connect to a live dashboard

All scan data flows into a real-time operations dashboard. Full history, full auditability, zero paperwork.

05

Train, refine, and scale

Hands-on team training followed by iterative improvement as data matures. Built to scale as you grow.
